1. All video submissions must have a time duration of minimum 30 seconds and a maximum of 59 seconds.
2. It is strictly compulsory for your video to have facts related to Space or Astronomy only.
3. There is no specific frame size for your videos.
4. You can use Adobe premiere pro, After effects, Filmmora, Davinci resolve, or any other editing software to create your videos.
5. Your submission can support any of the following video formats, like MP4, MPEG, MPE, MPG (MPEG-1, MPEG-2), M2V (DVD-compliant MPEG-2), MTS (AVCHD), MOV, FLV, AVI.
6. Deadline for submission of short video entries will be on the 26th of December at 11.59 P.M.
7. This competition is only for Sri Lankan university category participants. Only entries of participants who are undergraduates in any Sri Lankan University till the 26th of December 2020, will be accepted.
8. Incomplete video submission applications will be discarded.
9. We will not accept entries that may contain any inappropriate, offensive, unrelated or illegal content. Such videos will be disqualified.
10. The most popular and most creative videos will be awarded.
11. Most creative award will give based on the decision of the judge panel. The judgesâ€™ decisions will be final.
12. Most popular award will be given based on the highest number of fair likes received for the videos.
13. A vote will be considered valid, only if the voter has also liked the SEDS Mora Facebook page.
14. In voting, using voting generators is illegal and the participant will be disqualified immediately.
